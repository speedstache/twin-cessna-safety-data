# Narrative Summary

On October 12, 2023, the Cessna 414 landed at Harry P. Williams Memorial Airport in Patterson, Louisiana, after a short flight from Gonzales. The airplane remained at idle while a passenger boarded for a subsequent flight to Sugar Land, Texas. Video showed the pilot exiting and inspecting something on the left side of the airplane before boarding again and taxiing for departure. Shortly after takeoff, video captured the airplane descending in an extreme nose-low attitude while rolling through inverted. A witness saw the airplane tilted left, turn on its side, enter a nosedive, and impact a field. Examination found no airframe malfunction that would have precluded normal operation. The elevator trim was found in a significant nose-up position, consistent with the landing setting from the prior arrival. The airplane flight manual before-takeoff checklist required the trim tabs to be set. Both occupants were fatally injured and the airplane was destroyed.
