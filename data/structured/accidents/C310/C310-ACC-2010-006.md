# Narrative Summary

The pilot stated that he used the checklist and moved the landing gear handle to the down position before landing at Boerne Stage Field.

He also stated that he did not check the annunciator lights, and the airplane landed on partially extended landing gear.

Runway scrapes indicated that all three landing gears were only partially extended when the airplane touched down.

Postaccident examination found that the landing gear circuit breaker had opened, and the nose gear and right main gear could be pulled down manually and locked in position.

The left main landing gear could not be extended because of spar damage, but testing of the squat switch revealed no anomalies.
