# Narrative Summary

The pilot had purchased the airplane that day and conducted a test flight before planning to return to his home airport. The flight and landing were normal. During the landing rollout, the pilot applied brakes to slow and make the next taxiway turnoff. The nose wheel began to shimmy during the turn, applying side loads to the nose landing gear linkage and structure. The pilot taxied the airplane to a hangar and then observed structural damage. The pilot reported no preimpact mechanical malfunctions or failures that would have precluded normal operation.
