# Narrative Summary
During instructional operations, the pilot executed a hard landing and bounce during a prior touch-and-go. After departure, a landing gear indication anomaly was noted. Multiple low passes were conducted to visually verify gear position. The right main landing gear subsequently collapsed during landing roll. Examination revealed a bent push-pull tube consistent with a prior hard landing. Removal of the damaged component restored system function. The airplane sustained substantial damage; no injuries occurred.
