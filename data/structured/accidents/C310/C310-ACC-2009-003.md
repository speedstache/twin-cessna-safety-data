# Narrative Summary
While taxiing onto the runway to begin takeoff, the right main landing gear collapsed during a 90-degree turn. The right wing and horizontal stabilizer contacted the ground. An FAA inspector identified a missing pivot pin in the side brace lock link. The aircraft had flown minimal hours over the preceding years and had completed an annual inspection nine days earlier. No injuries occurred. The aircraft sustained substantial damage.
