# Narrative Summary

The pilot reported normal preflight and ground operations and departed with the fuel tanks filled to capacity. Several minutes after departure at 5,800 feet mean sea level, the left engine lost power. The pilot initiated emergency checklist procedures and requested right turns only for a return to the departure airport. Shortly thereafter, the right engine also lost power, and changing to the auxiliary tanks did not restore engine response. The pilot performed a gear-up forced landing in a farm field, and a postaccident fire consumed portions of the left wing. Examination found no fuel system malfunction, contamination, or engine anomaly that would have precluded normal operation.
