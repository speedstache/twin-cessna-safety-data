# Narrative Summary
During a night IFR approach in reported fog conditions, the pilot executed a second approach after an initial missed attempt. Radar and GPS data indicated descent below published minimum descent altitude. The aircraft impacted terrain approximately three-quarters of a mile from the runway. Engines were producing power at impact and no mechanical anomalies were identified. Toxicology revealed multiple medications. The pilot was fatally injured.
