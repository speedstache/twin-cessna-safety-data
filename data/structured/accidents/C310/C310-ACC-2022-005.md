# Narrative Summary

The pilot stated that the landing was firm but not hard enough to damage the airplane. After brake application, the airplane pulled to the right, and the pilot steered it toward the grass. The airplane struck a runway sign as it departed the runway to the right. The right main landing gear collapsed, and the right wing contacted the ground. After the airplane was lifted, the right main landing gear extended and the downlock was found broken. Postaccident examination of the downlock and right main landing gear revealed no anomalies that would have precluded normal operation.
