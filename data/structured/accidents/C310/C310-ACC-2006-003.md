# Narrative Summary

During landing rollout on runway 28R at Billings Logan International Airport, the right main landing gear collapsed following a normal approach and touchdown. The pilot reported that all three landing gear were verified down and locked prior to landing. Visual meteorological conditions prevailed at the time of the accident.

The aircraft sustained substantial damage. The commercial pilot and two passengers were not injured. The reason for the landing gear collapse was not determined during the investigation.

This was a part 135 on demand air taxi flight.
