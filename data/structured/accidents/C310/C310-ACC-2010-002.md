# Narrative Summary

The pilot accelerated the airplane on a 3,000-foot grass airstrip for departure from Ashdown, Arkansas.

He reported that heavy rain began suddenly during the takeoff roll and that he attempted to abort the takeoff when visibility was lost.

The airplane exited the airstrip and struck a mobile home, and the fuselage sustained structural damage.

The pilot reported that there were no mechanical failures or malfunctions with the airplane.

The report stated that the pilot had not received instruction in the accident make and model and did not hold a multiengine rating at the time of the accident.
