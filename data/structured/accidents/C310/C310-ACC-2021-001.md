# Narrative Summary

While on downwind, the pilot lowered the landing gear handle and verbally announced that the gear was down. He looked at the cockpit indications but then shifted attention outside the cockpit to scan for other traffic entering the pattern. The pilot reported that he did not confirm the landing gear was down and locked. During landing, the bottom of the fuselage sustained substantial damage. A postaccident functional test of the landing gear revealed no mechanical anomalies. The pilot reported no preimpact mechanical malfunctions or failures with the airframe or engines.
