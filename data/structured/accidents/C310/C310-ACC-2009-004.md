# Narrative Summary
The pilot departed under IFR with a 300-foot overcast and one-mile visibility. Shortly after takeoff, ATC lost radio contact. Witnesses observed the aircraft descending out of clouds in a right bank at high power. The airplane impacted a house approximately four miles from the airport. Examination revealed no preimpact mechanical anomalies. The environmental conditions and flight path were consistent with spatial disorientation.
