# Narrative Summary
After departure, the pilot experienced a simultaneous landing gear and cabin heater anomaly. Attempts to recycle the landing gear were unsuccessful. Ground personnel confirmed the right main gear remained retracted. The pilot elected to land with the landing gear retracted. Postaccident examination revealed failure of the rod end of the up-lock push-pull tube. The part had been discarded prior to laboratory evaluation. The aircraft sustained substantial damage; no injuries occurred.
