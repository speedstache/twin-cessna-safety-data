# Narrative Summary

The airplane was on final approach with the landing gear extended when the cockpit gear indication light flickered and then became solid. The pilot visually verified that all three landing gear were extended and continued to land. Upon touchdown, the left main landing gear collapsed and the airplane slid off the runway into an adjacent field. Postaccident examination found missing cotter pins and hardware, improper landing gear downlock tension, and a mis-rigged landing gear actuator. The most recent landing gear maintenance had been documented 15 days before the accident. The NTSB determined that improper maintenance of the landing gear system resulted in the partial gear collapse during landing.
