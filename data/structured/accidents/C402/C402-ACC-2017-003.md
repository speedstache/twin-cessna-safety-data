# Narrative Summary

The pilot departed Hyannis, Massachusetts, in a Cessna 402C.
During initial climb at night, about 800 ft above ground level, the airplane encountered a flock of birds.
The pilot declared an emergency with the tower.
The airplane returned and landed without further incident.
The airplane sustained substantial damage to the windscreen and windscreen frame.
The pilot reported no preaccident mechanical failures or malfunctions with the airplane.
The NTSB determined that the event resulted from an encounter with a flock of birds during takeoff at night.
