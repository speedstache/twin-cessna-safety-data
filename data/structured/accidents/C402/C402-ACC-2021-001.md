# Narrative Summary

During descent about five nautical miles from New Bedford, Massachusetts, the pilot observed a bird flying from left to right. The bird entered a dive and struck the airplane's vertical stabilizer. The pilot did not observe any flight anomalies after the impact. The pilot continued to the destination airport and landed without further incident. Subsequent examination revealed substantial damage to the vertical stabilizer. The pilot was not injured.
