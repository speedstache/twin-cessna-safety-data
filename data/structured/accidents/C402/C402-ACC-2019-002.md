# Narrative Summary

The airplane was on short final to St. Louis Lambert International Airport during a Part 135 non-scheduled flight.
The operator's director of safety reported that the pilot maneuvered quickly to avoid a bird.
After the pilot rolled wings level, a second bird went through the propeller.
The bird then struck the horizontal stabilizer.
The pilot landed the airplane without further incident.
The airplane sustained substantial damage to the left horizontal stabilizer.
The director of safety reported no preaccident mechanical failures or malfunctions with the airplane.
