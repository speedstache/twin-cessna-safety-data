# Narrative Summary

During initial climb after departure from Barnstable, Massachusetts, the crew hatch door warning light illuminated. The crew hatch door opened, and the forward hinge assembly separated from its fuselage mount point with adjacent aircraft skin. The airplane sustained substantial damage, and the pilot was not injured. Postaccident examination found that the door safety latch, warning system, and door latching mechanism operated properly. The operator reported that the door could be latched with the forward pin in its proper anchor point while the aft pin remained outside the airframe and the warning light extinguished. The operator reported that the issue was later addressed in aircrew training and company standard operating procedures.
