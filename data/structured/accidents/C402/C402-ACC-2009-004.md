# Narrative Summary

Before a Part 135 cargo flight, the pilot found the airplane parked into the sun with condensation on the windscreen.
The pilot attempted to clear the condensation by hand, but it reappeared.
The pilot began taxi with the left crew door open to improve visibility.
While turning onto the cargo ramp, the airplane’s left wing and propeller struck an unattended parked airplane.
The taxiing airplane sustained substantial damage to the left wing structure and the parked airplane sustained structural damage to the forward nose area.
After both engines were secured, the pilot exited unassisted.
