# Narrative Summary

The airplane was being repositioned under Part 91 and entered the traffic pattern for landing at the destination airport.
Radar data indicated the airplane maneuvered in the pattern and then turned to final approach.
During the final leg, the airplane crashed short of the runway into a residential area.
The report notes published minimum multi-engine approach speed and minimum controllable airspeed values and indicates the airplane’s groundspeed was low on final with a likely crosswind component.
The pilot was fatally injured and the airplane sustained substantial damage.
Further causal detail was not available in the extracted summary text used for this record.
