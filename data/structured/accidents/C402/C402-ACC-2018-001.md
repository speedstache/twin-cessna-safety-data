# Narrative Summary

The pilot landed at Mount Sterling, Kentucky, after rain had recently occurred.
The airplane touched down on the first third of the wet runway on the centerline.
During the landing roll, the pilot allowed the airplane to continue toward the runway end to avoid excessive braking.
As he turned left toward a taxiway, he noticed that the brakes were not very effective.
The airplane began to pivot right, and the right main landing gear and nosewheel slid.
The airplane exited the runway, and the nosewheel bumped and folded.
The airplane came to rest nose down with substantial fuselage damage.
The pilot reported no preaccident mechanical failures or malfunctions with the airplane.
