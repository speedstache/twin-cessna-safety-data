# Narrative Summary

On a Part 135 scheduled passenger flight, the pilot conducted a visual approach in marginal VMC.
During descent to the traffic pattern the pilot observed the left airspeed indication decrease rapidly from about 145 knots to about 85 knots.
The pilot applied full power but did not see an increase in indicated airspeed and elected to continue the approach.
After configuring flaps and landing gear, the pilot perceived the airplane was traveling faster than indicated.
The airplane touched down long, bounced, and the pilot reported braking action was nil due to runway contamination.
The airplane departed the paved surface and came to rest beyond the runway end, and the cause of the airspeed anomaly could not be determined.
