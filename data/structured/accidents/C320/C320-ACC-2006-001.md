# Narrative Summary

During takeoff from runway 25R at Long Beach, the pilot reported setting the flight controls for a crosswind takeoff. After rotation, the airplane did not climb as expected and the left wing was slightly low. As the pilot turned into the wind, the right wing dropped and the right wing tip fuel tank contacted the runway. The pilot stabilized the airplane, reduced power to idle, and stopped beyond the runway. The pilot reported no mechanical malfunctions with the airplane. The reported wind was from 180 degrees at 7 knots. The FAA inspection noted damage to the rear spar at the right wing tip.
