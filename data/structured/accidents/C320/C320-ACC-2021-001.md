# Narrative Summary

The pilot departed Sherman Municipal Airport after filling the airplane six fuel tanks for a flight to Lubbock. About 20 minutes after departure, the pilot detected a partial loss of power on the right engine and noticed a drop in right-engine fuel flow. Activating the electric boost pump restored power briefly before the right engine again lost power. The pilot reported that he did not switch fuel tanks before shutting down and feathering the right engine. He diverted toward Olney Municipal Airport, but the airplane could not maintain altitude. The pilot performed a forced landing to a field, during which the airplane struck trees and sustained substantial damage. Postaccident examination found no anomalies with the right engine, and the engine fuel controls were not available for examination.
