# Narrative Summary

The aircraft experienced a loss of engine power during cruise flight approximately 45 minutes after departure. The pilot reported abnormal engine indications and elected to divert to a nearby airport.

The investigation revealed that maintenance had been performed on the affected engine’s fuel system two days prior to the flight. Examination identified improper torque application on a fuel system fitting, resulting in fuel leakage and power loss.

The pilot secured the affected engine and conducted a single-engine landing without further incident. The aircraft sustained minor damage during rollout.

The investigation attributed the power loss to improper maintenance procedures.
