# Narrative Summary

The pilot had used a self-serve fuel station at Bend Municipal Airport. While taxiing after fueling, the airplane’s left wingtip fuel tank struck a post at the fuel station. A fire ignited after the collision. The pilot shut down both engines and exited the airplane with the passenger. The left wing and fuselage sustained substantial damage. The pilot and passenger were not injured. The pilot reported no preaccident mechanical malfunction or failure that would have precluded normal operation. The NTSB attributed the accident to the pilot’s failure to maintain clearance from the fuel station.
