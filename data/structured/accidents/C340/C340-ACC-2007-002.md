# Narrative Summary

During a local dual proficiency flight, the crew conducted multiple approaches and landings before the accident landing at Middleton Municipal Airport.
On final, the pilot observed three green gear indications and the landing gear warning horn was silent.
After a light touchdown, the airplane began to shudder and the gear horn sounded during the landing roll.
The right main landing gear indication extinguished, the red unsafe light illuminated, and the right main gear collapsed as the airplane drifted right.
The airplane departed the runway pavement, struck two runway lights, and came to rest in a grass area adjacent to the runway.
Postaccident examination found the forward bellcrank attachment flange on the landing gear strut failed, and the lower flange on the bellcrank also failed.
Maintenance records showed a recent annual inspection included landing gear inspection and adjustment, but an improper adjustment procedure could not be attributed to a specific mechanic.
